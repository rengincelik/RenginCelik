Featured Project

PipeFlow
Simulator

A general-purpose pipeline flow simulator for process engineers. Build a pipeline from components ? pumps, pipes, valves, elbows, reducers ? and the physics engine calculates pressure, velocity, flow rate, and energy losses every 100ms. Results appear as live SVG animations, stacked charts, and component-level tooltips.
